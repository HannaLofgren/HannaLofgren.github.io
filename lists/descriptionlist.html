Descriptions-typography

Linotype
Line-casting allowed type to be selected, used then recirculated into the machine automatically.
This advance increased the speed of typsetting and printing dramatically.
photocomposition
Typefaves are stored on film then projected onto photo-sensitive paper.
Lenses adjust the size of the type.
Digital type

Digital typefaces store the outline of the font shape in a format such as Postscript.
The outline may be scaled to any size for output.
